Europe


Europe is the world's second-smallest continent by surface area, covering about 10,180,000 km2 (3,930,000 miles2) or 2% of the Earth's surface anda bout 6.8% of it's land area. Of Europe's approximately 50 countries, Russia is by far the largest by both area and population, taling up 40% of the continent (although the country has territory in both Europe and Asia), while Vatican City is the smallest. Europe is the third-most populous continent after Asia and Africa, with a population of 739-743 million or about 11% of the world's population.
