Africa


Africa is the world's second-most-populous continent. At about 30.2 million km2(11.7 million 2 miles) including adjacent islands, it covers 6% of Earth's area and 20.4% of it's total land area. With 1.1 billion people as of 2013, it accounts for about 1.5% of the world's human population. The continent is surrounded by the Mediterranean Sea to the north, both the Suez Canal and the Ocean to the west. The continent includes Madagascar and various aarchipelagos. It has 54 fully recognized sovereign states (or countries), nine territories and two de facto independent states with limited oor no recognition
